Ladies Ministry

The purpose of Life Church Men's Ministry is to be used by God to bring men to Christ and to help them become more like Christ.

We believe children should strive to:

  1. Know God personally
  2. Communicate with God Regularly
  3. Absorb's God's Word Daily
  4. Relate to others openly
  5. Follow God's call passionately
